Tiramisu

Description

In my house there is nothing that goes faster than a Tiramisu.

Ingredients

  • Lady fingers
  • Coffee
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Mascarpone
  • Cream
  • Cocoa

Steps

  1. In a large bowl with a hand mixer or using the whisk attachment on a stand mixer beat together the sugar and the egg (if using) until frothy, then add the mascarpone and cream, beat until thickened.
  2. In the baking dish place a coffee dipped ladyfinger on the bottom, top with 1/3 of the mascarpone mixture and continue for another two layers, finish with the remaining mascarpone mixture.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 2-3 hours or even better overnight. Just before serving sprinkle with unsweetened cocoa powder.
